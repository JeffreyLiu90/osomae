Welcome to O'Somae

We here at O’SOMAE are hoping to provide the community with a new and exciting brunch option that includes many non-traditional ingredients that will surely excite all types of foodies. Our café offers fresh indigenous ingredients, promoting local farms and businesses. All of our dishes promote from-scratch cooking. Aside from the food, we also brew each cup of coffee with heart and passion using only the best coffee beans, you will sure taste the difference.
